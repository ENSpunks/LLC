[Delaware Limited Liability Company Act](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title6/c018/index.html)

[ยง 18-305(d)](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title6/c018/sc03/index.html): A limited liability company may maintain its records in other than a written form, including on, by means of, or in the form of any information storage device, method, or 1 or more electronic networks or databases (including 1 or more distributed electronic networks or databases), if such form is capable of conversion into written form within a reasonable time.
